This is an islamic online coding club for Muslims and non-Muslims from all around the world. Muslim coding club was built so that developers all around the globe can connect and assist each other in coding. Here at Muslim Coding Club, we solve problems, work on projects, and learn new techonologies as one family. We do not dicriminate against other religions and you dont have to be muslim to join our club. There is no compulsion in Islam. The main reason that we nameed our club "Muslim Coding Club" is because we value the morals, rules, and virtues that islam teaches us about. Before joining our club, we would really appreciate you taking a minute and reading throuhg our rules page.
